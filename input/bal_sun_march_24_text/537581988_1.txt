Negro Youths Sentenced.
Norman Ball, Simon Levitzsky and Ells-
worth Parker, colored, three of the boys
arrested for robbing the Maryland Tele-
phone booth in the first corridor of the
Postoffice, pleaded guilty in the United
States District Court yesterday. Ball and
Levitzky were sentenced to three years
each in St. Mary's Industrial School. Par
ker was sent to the Colored House of Ref-
ormation at Cheltenham for one year.
