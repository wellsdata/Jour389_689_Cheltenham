Nice Orders Probe
In Cheltenham Riot
Harry C. Jones, Due Back Mon-
day, To Consider Disorder
Charges At Negro Home
Harry C. Jones, State Employment
Commissioner, will make an investiga-
tion next week of the riot which broke
out at the Cheltenham School for Boys
Thursday night, it was announced
yesterday, Governor Nice ordered the
investigation from his office in Annap-
olis. Mr. Jones, on vacation, will return
Monday.
Meanwhile, two ringleaders in the
revolt, it was learned at the Southern
Maryland Negro reformatory, have
been placed in solitary confinement
by order of B. B. Sunderland, superin-
tendent.
