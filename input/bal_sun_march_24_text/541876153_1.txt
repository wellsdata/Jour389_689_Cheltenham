Lawson J. Veney was installed as

school gymnasium near Upper
Marlboro,
Dr. William E. Henry, chair-

sided. The program consisted of
selections by two choruses from
school cottages, and addresses of
greeting and welcome delivered to
an audience of approximately 450.
Mrs. Bertha Proctor, a member
of the board of managers, gave
the welcoming address.

represented by William Wiggins.

Woodhouse, a retired school prin-
cipal.
