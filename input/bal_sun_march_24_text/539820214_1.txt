Charges Predicted Today In

gust Matt
QUARREL THEORY CITED
Victim Called From Home By
Assailant Previous To
Murder
[By the Associated Press]
Upper Marlboro, Md., Dec. 26-An
unidentified farmer was held today in
connection with the Christmas Eve
slaying of August Matt, 40. Police
said murder charges probably would
be filed tomorrow.
Matt was shot through the abdomen
by a man who drove a car into the
yard of his Cheltenham home, called
him by name and fired when Matt
came out of the house.
Quarrel Theory Advanced
Authorities worked on the theory
the slaying resulted from a quarrel
involving Mrs. Leta Bredhaute, said to
have been employed at one time as a
housekeeper by the suspect in the
shooting, and more recently engaged
in the same capacity by Matt.
Mrs. Brodhaute found Matt lying
on the ground mortally wounded after
the shooting.
Christmas Play Proves
Fatal To Child At York
[Special Dispatch to The Sun]
York, Pa., Dec. 26-Christmas joys
were turned to sorrow today at the
home of Mr. and Mrs. Jacob Sweitzer,
when their son, Richard, 20 months,
died from injuries sustained in a fall
while playing with one of his toys.
While the boy was running with a
two-wheeled toy, he tripped over a
raised floor board, his head striking
a rocking chair on which his mother
sat with an infant in her arms.
The impact inflicted a fractured
skull and brain concussion, which
proved fatal.
Counsel For Herrera
Gets Habeas Corpus Writ
Hearing On Plea Of Former Cuban
Leader Is Set For
Tomorrow
New York, Dec. 26 (AP)-Counsel for
Alberto Herrera, who was President
of Cuba for a few hours after Gerardo
Machado resigned, today obtained a
writ of habeas corpus in a move to
seek freedom for Herrera.
The former Cuban President, who
tis wanted by the present Cuban Gov-
ernment on a killing charge, was ar-
rested in a French hospital on a war-
rant issued by Garrett W. Cotter,
United States Commissioner, at the
request of counsel for the Cuban
Government.
Herrera is suffering from a com-
pound fracture of the skull received
sin an automobile accident. The writ
is returnable before Federal Judge
Alfred C. Coxe Friday.
