At present the charter reads: "The

shall be appointed by the Governor, in

the Legislature to amend the above so
as to read:
"The estate and concerns of said cor-
poration shall be managed and con-
ducted by eighteen managers, of whom
six shall be elected by members of the
association and six appointed by the
Mayor and City Council of Baltimore-

nually."
