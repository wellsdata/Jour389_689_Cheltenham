ESCAPED AND WALKED HERE
John Wesley Young Said He Was
In Cheltenham.
Footsore and weary and with his hands
and feet cut and bruised, John Wesley
Young, a 16-year-old negro, who says he
escaped from the Cheltenham Reformatory
last Thursday, was found exhausted near
the Pennsylvania railroad near Fulton Sta-
lion yesterday morning about 11 o'clock by
Sergeant Pfennig, of the Northwestern dis-
trict. He said he had walked all the way
to Baltimore and finally became so weak
that he stumbled and fell.
Sergeant Pfennig had the boy removed to
the Maryland Homeopathic Hospital. He
will be held pending word from the Chel-
tenham authorities. Young's mother lives
at 1147 North Calhoun street.
