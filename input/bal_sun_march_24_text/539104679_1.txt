Lions Club Gift Used
For Equipment For Boys
Of a $500 gift by the Baltimore
(Downtown) Lions Club to the Bal-
timore Youth Commission, $250 has
been used to finance and equip a
a drum and bugle corps at the
Cheltenham School for Boys, Judge
Charles E. Moylan, of the Supreme
Bench of Baltimore and commis-
sion chairman, said yesterday in a
letter to the club.
An additional $28 was used to
buy soft balls and bats for the boys
at the Maryland Training School
for Boys, the jurist said.
Baltimore Lions, his letter added,
"can be very proud of the fact that
they are helping in the rehabilita
tion of the boys at both schools. 1
happen to know that the boys them-
selves are most appreciative of
these needed gifts.".
