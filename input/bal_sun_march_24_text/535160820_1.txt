Boy Sent To Cheltenham.
Louis Williams, colored, 14 years old,
1072 Raborg street, and Thomas Queen,
colored, 9 years old, 1074 Raborg street,
arrested Friday on the charge of breaking
into the warehouse of L. H. Newton &
Son, Raborg and Woodyear streets, and
stealing baseballs and other articles worth
$5.65, faced Judge Williams, in the Juv-
enile Court, yesterday. The older boy
was sent to the House of Reformation at
Cheltenham, while Queen was released un-
der the supervision of a probation oficer
of the court.
