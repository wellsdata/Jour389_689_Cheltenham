Strike Of The Caddies.
The caddies at the Baltimore Country
Club became independent on Independence
Day and struck for 20 cents an hour, which
Is double the usual price forchasing around
with enthusiastic golfers and carrying bags
of golf clubs.
The players and the golf committee con-
cluded that the boys wanted to celebrate
the Fourth and paid little attention to the
strike, but some players were inconven-
lenced.
