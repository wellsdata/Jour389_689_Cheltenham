JOHN A. VOGLER.
Mr. John A. Vogler, 57 years old, died
Friday night from a complication of dis-
eases at his home, 1744 Ashland avenue.
Besides his wife, who before her marriage
was Miss Margaret Euler, he is survived
by a son, Mr. George J. Vogler.
The funeral will take place Tuesday aft-
crnoon. Rev. C. W. Locher, pastor of St.
Matthew's German Evangelical Lutheran
Church, Fayette street and Central avenue,
will conduct the services.
ROBERT LAUTENBACH.
Mr. Robert Lautenbach, a well-known
druggist at Eutaw and Saratoga streets,
died yesterday at his home, 100 McMechen
street, after a lingering illness from pul-
monary troubles. He conducted for many
years the drug store, which was established
by his father, the late Robert Lautenbach,
at the same location in 1867.
Besides his wife, Mr. Lautenbach is sur-
vived by four children. The funeral will
take place from the house Tuesday.
