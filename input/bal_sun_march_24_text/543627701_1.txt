Negro Sentenced As Minor
Taken From Cheltenham
Accused, Sald To Be 21, Gave Age
As 15-Convicted in At-
tempted Robbery.
The alleged deception of a 21-year-
old Negro, who gave his age as 15
when arrested for attempted burglary,
was discovered yesterday after he had
been sentenced to the House of
Reformation for Colored Boys at Chel-
tenham. Richard L. Mitchell, the ac-
cused, was removed to the Northwest-
Tern Police Station for a new trial.
■ The accused, who gave an address
in the first block East Belvedere ave-
nue, was charged February 15 with
breaking into the store of Joe Cohen,
1000 block North Stricker street.
Magistrate T. J. C. Williams, in the
Juvenile Court, sentenced Mitchell to
Cheltenham, thinking him a minor.
The police said they obtained his
birth certificate from a Connecticut
town. Magistrate Paul Johannsen
committed him to Jail in default of
$500 bail for grand jury action.
