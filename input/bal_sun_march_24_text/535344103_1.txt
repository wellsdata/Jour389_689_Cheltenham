ALL OUT FOR REAL SPEED
Laurel Has Motorcycle And Harness
And Running Horse Races.
[Special Dispatch to the Baltimore Sun.]
| Laurel, Md., July 3.-The Laurel Four-
County Fair race meet, which began Tues-
day last, 'will close tomorrow.
Today was an off day, as it was the de-
sire of the promoters to reserve a big card
for tomorrow.
To cater to the liking of different ad-
mirers of speed sport the card as made
could not be improved upon. It embraces
harness races, horse-running races and
motorcycle races.
