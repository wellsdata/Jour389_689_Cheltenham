Cheltenham To Get
$36,000 For Repairs
Approximately $36,000 has been
made available by the Board, of
Public Works to the Cheltenham
School for Boys for new refrigera-
tion and general repairs at the
Negro training school, it was an-
nounced yesterday.
J
Thomas B. Sprague, president of
the institution's board of man-
agers, said the improvements
would greatly simplify administra.
tion of the school and that efforts
were being made to get the work
underway as soon as possible.
