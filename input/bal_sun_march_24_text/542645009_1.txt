Luttrell Given
Cheltenham Post
J. Grayson Luttrell, vice presi-
dent of MeCormick & Co., Inc.,
has been named a member of the
board of managers of the Chelten-
ham School for Boys, it was an-
nounced yesterday at Governor
Lane's office.
He will fill the vacancy on the
board caused by the death of How-
ard C. Hill, educator and social
worker. Mr. Hill's term was to ex-
pire in 1951.
Mr. Luttrell is a former presi-
dent of the Rotary Club of Balti-
more and is a director of the Fed-
eral Home Loan Bank of Winston-
Salem, N.C.
He is recognized as a tea expert
and is president of the Tea Associ-
ation of the United States.
