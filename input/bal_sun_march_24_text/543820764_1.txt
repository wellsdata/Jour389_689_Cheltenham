To Investigate Fairs.
Another committee was appointed to
investigate street carnivals and fairs
and to ascertain the character of the

Several speakers declared that, al-
though numerous carnivals were given
throughout the city, ostensibly for
churches, the larger part of the pro-
ceeds was given to the private indi-
viduals who conduct them.
