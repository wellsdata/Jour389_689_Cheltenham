SENTENCED FOR PAPER THEFT
Negro Boy Charged With Taking
Copies Of The Sun.
Robert Johnson, colored, 14 years old,
1714 North Mount street, was sentenced
to the Colored House of Reformation,
at Cheltenham. by Judge Luthardt, in
the Juvenile Court, yesterday a few
hours after he had been arrested for
stealing 25 copies of THE SUN. The bun-
dle of papers belonged to Louis Rosen,
2308 East Baltimore street and was
taken from the corner of Fulton and
North avenues, where it had been left for
him. Johnson had been in the Juvenile
Court before, a charge of larceny against
him having been dismissed on June 12,
