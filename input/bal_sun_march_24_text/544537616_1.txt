PAPER THIEVES PUNISHED
One To Be Sent Out Of City-Three
Are Fined.
Foar colored boys were before Judge
Williams, in the Juvenile Court yes-
terday, on two charges, one of them in-
volving an alleged theft of two copies
of THE SUNDAY SUN...
Thomas Compher, 13 years old, 1026
West Saratoga street, one of the four,
will be committed to Cheltenham an-
less he is sent out of the city by to-
morrow. His brother, James Compher,
14 years old; Clayton Roberts, 14 years.
old, 306 North Arlington avenue, and
David McCoy, 15 years old, 228 Bow-
ers Court, were each fined $2.90, the
costs in the case. The newspapers were
taken from bundles of papers at Fre-
mont avenue and Franklin street.
