Horse Meat Ruled Out
Prohibited sale of horse meat for
human consumption; extended un-
employment benefits from twenty
to twenty-three weeks; raised the
weekly maximum from $17 to $20;
set up a rebate system for em-
ployers with "good" employment
records; fixed imprisonment limits
for non-payment of fines-up to
thirty days for $100, sixty days up
to $500, ninety days over $500;
authorized prison labor use on
farms; provided transfer of judges
to other circuits if needed.
