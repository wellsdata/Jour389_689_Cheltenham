Legislator Is Victim
Of Spinal Meningitis
Luther P. Jefferson, 35, Was Mem.
ber Of House Of Delegates
From Cecil
Elkton, Md., Aug. 8 (M)-Spinal
meningitis today proved fatal to
Luther P. Jefferson, 35, member of
the House of Delegates from Cecil
county and a well-known farmer,
live-stock dealer and auctioneer.
Mr. Jefferson, who had been ill only
a short time, died in Union Memorial
Hospital, Baltimore.
Surviving are his wife, his father,
James Jefferson, of Chesapeake City,
and a sister, Mrs. Reginald Walworth,
of Hartford, Conn.
Funeral services will be held here
tomorrow afternoon, Burial will be
in Cecil County Cemetery, near Chesa-
peake City.
