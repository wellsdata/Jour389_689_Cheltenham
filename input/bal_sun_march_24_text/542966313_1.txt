State Funds Available
To Hospital And School
Annapolis, Feb. 12 (Special) -
State funds totaling $33,313 were
made available today for the
Spring Grove State Hospital and
the Cheltenham School for Boys.
Of $27,735 for labor and material
items at the hospital, $8,835 will
be used to pay overtime and
bonuses to employes in accordance
with a recent request by hospital
officials for additional wage funds
for attendants, farm hands, cooks
and steam firemen.
The allotment was made from
unused funds in the hospital's
budget which resulted from inabil-
ity to obtain much-needed replace-
ments for mechanical equipment
and repair work.
Of $5,578 made available for
Cheltenham, $3,500 will be trans-
ferred to the live-stock feed ac-
I count of the school.
