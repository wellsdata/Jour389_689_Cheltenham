In Cell When Father Was Buried.
Frank Furlong and Charles Mason were
seen entering a second-hand store, Sunday
by Patrolman Sedicum, of the Southwest-
Cern police district, and attempted to dispose
- of a mackintosh, for 50 cents.
They were taken to the police station,
and it was learned that Furlong's father
was dead and the coat had been taken from
the home. As the funeral took place yes-
terday morning the men were held until
yesterday afternoon, and were then, dis-
missed as the charge was not pressed
against them."
