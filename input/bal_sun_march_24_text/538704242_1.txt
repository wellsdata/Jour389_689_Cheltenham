New Sites Considered
For Cheltenham School
With the collapse of the deal for
acquisition of a 12242-acre tract be-
tween Mount Winans and Cherry
Hill, attributed to a "defective"
title, the board of managers of the
Cheltenham School for Boys has
several new sites in mind, Thomas
B. Sprague, board president, said
yesterday.
The State had agreed to pay
$45,000 for the property, but did
not consummate the contract of
sale when the title disclosed that
a projected highway to link the city
and the new airport would virtually
cut the Mount Winans-Cherry Hill
site in two. Only a down-payment
binder had been made.
