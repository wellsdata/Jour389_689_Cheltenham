Another Football Denth.
Philadelphia, March 4.- William Alte-
mus, aged 21 years, of Cheltenham, a sub-
urb, died in a hospital yesterday from
a broken back. The injury was received
last Thanksgiving Day while playing foot-
ball. The game in which Altemus was in-
jured was between Lawndale and Bristol,
suburban teams. He played a back field
position on the Lawndale team. A few
minutes after the game began Altemus
made a lying tackle, and in the mix-up
was rendered unconscious. He was re-
moved to the hospital, where he lingered
until today.
