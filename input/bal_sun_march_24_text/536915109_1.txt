General Riggs and the Mayor have not
been on intimate terms since the late
trouble in the School Board, which resulted
in the resignation of General Riggs from
the presidency of the board, Should he be
dropped as a director of the Maryland
School for Boys it is said he will be elected s
to a vacancy in the board on the part of
the subscribers and remain as the president.
a position which is filled by the managers,
and not by the Mayor.
The Mayor is also to appoint a minority
member of the Board of Commissioners for
Opening Streets, a member of the Park
Board, to succeed Mr. Douglas M. Wylle;
members of the Board of Supervisors of
City Charities, who are to be retained; the
Municipal Hospital Commission, the mem
bers of which are to be reappointed, and
the Commission on City Plan, which will
also be reappointed.
