3 Cheltenham School
Fugitives Are Found
Annapolis, Nov. 20 (AP)-City
police said today three youths who
escaped from Cheltenham School
Thursday have been found in
Dover, Del., along with a missing
car.
Police Sergeant Walter Muster-
man left for Dover to return the
boys, none older than 16, who were
reported seen in the automobile on
an eastbound Chesapeake Bay
ferry two nights ago.
Dermott V. Hickey, of Annapolis,
said the car had been taken from
outside a garage here.
