Four Cheltenham
Fugitives Caught
Four of the six youths who led
police a 100-block automobile chase
through the city yesterday morning
were escaped inmates of the Chel-
tenham School for Boys, and three
of them have been returned there,
Judge Charles E. Moylan, of the
division of juvenile causes, said last
night.
The six youths, all Negro juve-
niles, were given hearings before
Judge Moylan after their arrest
near Calvert and Twentieth streets
following a 75-mile-an-hour chase
from Fairûeld.
Judge Moylan said the fourth
Cheltenham inmate evidently was
the ring-leader and with the ap-
proval of the school authorities.
was being held for criminal prose-
cution.
One of the remaining two youths
was placed on probation and the
other will be committeed to the
Crownsville State Hospital.
