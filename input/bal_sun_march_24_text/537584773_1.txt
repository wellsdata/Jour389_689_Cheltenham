F. Reed Isaac
To Head Board
Of Cheltenham
+
F. Reid Isaac, superintendent of
the Strawbridge Home for Boys,
near Eldersburg, has been named
president of the board of managers
of the Cheltenham School for Boys.
He will assume his new duties
immediately.
Mr. Isaac was selected by the
board of managers of Cheltenham
at a meeting in the office of Harry
C. Jones, State employment com-
missioner. He will succeed Joseph
H. Blandford, who resigned in
April of this year, when members
of the board decided to abolish all
restrictions segregating the insti
tution's Negro employes from the
white.
The board also elected Joseph C.
Briscoe, Negro, principal of the
Negro Vocational School, vice
president of the board, and re
elected Hal T. Claggett, secretary-
treasurer.
