Original Complaint
In its original complaint against
the Spevak firm, the State OPA
charged the meat company with
nineteen violations of the price ceil-
ing regulations. Alan Carruthers,
special OPA hearing officer, upheld
the first ten allegations, which in-
volved sales of meat to army posts
in this area, and disallowed, the re-
maining counts in the first com-
plaint.
While the first nineteen charges
were awaiting hearing, Mr. Leon-
ard explained, OPA investigators
again checked the Spevak concern
and in a second complaint charged
the firm with fourteen other alleged
violations, thirteen of which were
sustained by Mr. Carruthers.
