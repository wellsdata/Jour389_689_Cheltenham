TO DISCUSS CHELTENHAM
Group To Call On Governor
O'Conor Today
An inter-racial committee of the In-
ternational League for Peace and
Freedom, headed by Mrs. Asbury
Smith, will call on Governor O'Conor
today at Annapolis to discuss the op-
eration of the Cheltenham School for
Colored Girls and Boys.
The committee, it was said, expects
to discuss with the Governor the com-
position of the board of managers of
the institution.
