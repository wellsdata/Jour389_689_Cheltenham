MAYOR FILLS 3 VACANCIES
Dietrich Succeeds Beadenkopf And
Harlan Takes Shryock's Position.
Three vacancies of long standing were
filled yesterday by Mayor Jackson. An-
drew J. Dietrich, 4000 Greenway, was
appointed a member of the board of
managers of the Maryland Institute for
the Promotion of the Mechanic Arts, to
succeed the late George Beadenkopf.
Enoch Harlan, 2914 Garrison Boule-
vard, and Bailey Conaway (colored),
2457 MeCulloh street, were appointed
members of the board of managers of
the House of Reformation for Colored
Boys, Cheltenham.
Mr. Harlan succeeds the late Thomas
J. Shryock and Conaway takes the place
of E. B. Taylor. The appointees will
be asked to qualify tomorrow.
