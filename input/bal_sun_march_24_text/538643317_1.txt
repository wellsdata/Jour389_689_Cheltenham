CHELTENHAM BOARD SUES
Seeks To Recover Pay For 24
Dropped By Jones
Upper Marlboro, Md., May 3 (AP)-
The board of managers of the Chelten-
ham School for Boys filed a mandamus
suit today to recover salaries of
twenty-four employes of the institu-
tion.
The employes were cut off the pay
roll by Harry C. Jones, State Employ-
ment Commissioner, on the ground
they were unfit. The suit said that the
legislative act of 1937, which made the
school a State institution, provided
For blanketing in the present employes
without examination.
