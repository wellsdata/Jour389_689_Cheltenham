Extension Society.
The Reformed Church Extension Society
which was recently formed, completed its or-
ganization at the Third Church, Paca and
Saratoga streets, last night by electing the
following officers: President, Rev. A. S.
Weber; secretary. F. A. Diffenbaugh; treas-
urer, George W. Mower.
